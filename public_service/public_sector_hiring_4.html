What does Canadian International Development Agency do?

^

CIDA (now merged with Global Affairs) funds development projects. It administers the financial aspects of funding other organizations who do the projects.

What does International Development Agency Do?

^

IDRC does both direct research as well as helps to build the research capacity of developing countries

What does Immigration and Citizenship do?

^

Looks at ways to attract newcomers to Canada that are reuniting with relatives already here, coming as refugees, or are mobile labour that want to come to work in Canada

What does Canadian Security Intelligence Service do?

^

Analyis of global security threats to Canada. Processes security clearances for the rest of governmet (mostly)

What does Export Development Corporation do?

^

linked to Industry Canada and International Trade. Goal is to encourage exports abroad, often through catalytic financing

What does Employment and Social Development Canada do?

^

ESDC including Service Canada is the largest department outside of Defence and has three large statuatory programs, EI, CPP, and Old Age Security.

^

It also has numerous programs including Student Loans. $110 billion in programming and more than 20,000 employees across the country

What does Health Canada do?

^

Health Canada and Public Health Agency of Canada are responsible for a variety of health programs across Canada.

^

IT also manages transfers to Provinces and Territories. Long-term health research, epidemiological concerns, health promotion, and approval of all drugs

What do the RCMP do?

^

National Police force, mirroring many functions of the FBI in the U.S.A. They also accept contracts from Provinces, as such they have many responsibilities from high-level national crimes to terrorism, human trafficking, and cyber crime.

What does Canadian Border Services Agency do?

^

Secure Canada’s border against illicit traffic in both humans and contraband, at times working with passport office.

What do Justice and Correctional Services do?

^

Justice takes the lead for legislation and legal cases. Correctional Services plays strong role in simple containment, but also does some policy work on rehabilitation.

What are the sectoral departments?

^

Agriculture, Agri-Food Canada, Canadian Food Inspection Agency, Environment Canada, Natural Resources Canada, Parks Canada, Fisheries, Canadian Heritage (Citizenship, museums), Transport Canada

Is a single page resume to your advantage when applying for a government job?

^

No. A single page resume can get you a job in the private sector, but in the Public Sector it will almost certainly get you screened out.

What does legislation dictate precisely for all government job competitions?

^

Canadian government has legislation that defines precisely what ’merit’ means for all competitions.

^

A manager must be able to document and substantiate ’HOW’ a person demonstrates merit and ’WHY“ they are the right person
