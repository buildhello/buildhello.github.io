Public Sector Hiring 3

What is AS classification?

^

AS refers to Administration. They are internal facing enabling services, like human resource and finance.

  • Accounting, auditing, hiring, payroll, records management, IT,

Describe research phase for a potential crown corporation?

^

Literature review, case studies, informational interviews, statistical trends, comparative analysis, market position assessment, all pulled together into a ’diagnostique’

Describe the analysis stage for a potential crown corporation.

^

Risk assessment, mitigation strategies, proposals, consideration of instruments, etc..

all leading to a specific response or set of responses to meet current need.

What process ensures policies are palatable to Ministers and the political sphere?

^

There is ongoing and continuous and plentiful back-and-forth between civil servants and ministers that effectively serve to weed out any truly ’unacceptable’ or ’unpalatable’ recommendations

Give an example of a program that might require project managers

^

Small project program that helps fund market assessment for overseas markets to identify sales niches and boost revenues.

^

Companies apply, government PMs assess applications/proposals, some get funded, private sector does market assessments, reports are issued, monies tracked, and files closed.

admin, accounting, finance background,

Do people in government percieve a hierarchy that doesn’t exist?

^

Many people in government percieve falsly that ECs are more senior/important than PMs and that PMs are more senior/important than AS.

^

The misperception is partly born out of bias around educational requirements.

Do ECs typically make more money than PMs and ASs?

^

ECs tend to have a higher starting salary and working level, ECs tend to be EC-04, EC-05, while there are huge numbers of PM and AS positions at -01 and -02 levels ECs also get promoted more quickly.

^

If you are EC-02, you might jump to EC-04, EC-05 in a couple of years, but AS-01 and AS-02 may not move up to AS-04, or -05 level quickly
