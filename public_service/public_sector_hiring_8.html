What does reliabililty and security mean?

^

Your security clearance. Reliability is a simple background check for criminal records, “Enhanced reliability,” “Secret,” and “Top Secret”.

What does ’willing and available to work overtime’ mean?

^

Once a month, once a year, occasional overtime, planned overtime or sudden overtime.

How many lines of text in how many paragraphs are required to prove an experience requirement in a cover letter?

^

For most experience requirements, 8-10 lines of text in 1-2 paragraphs.

^

If the Merit Criteria require 4 experience requirements, add two paragraphs on eligibility, plus salutation and closing is 6 - 10 paragraphs
