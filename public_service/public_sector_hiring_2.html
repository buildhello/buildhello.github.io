^

What type of person does not typically like working a government job?

^

Strong outcome-oriented people who like to see the direct visible result of their personal work and initiative tend to be the unhappiest in government

What is the difference between a crown corporation and government department?

^

NGOs, educational organizations, health care providers, and crown corporations operate like businesses.

^

Government departments are not profit oriented, they contain predictable but different power structures, and incentives
