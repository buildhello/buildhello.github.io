Profile: Yuri Tricys

Little by little, a little becomes a lot.




Hello, Dear Readers!

My name is Yuri. I'm a freelance web developer, and the current maintainer writer/web developer for this newsletter.


Web development is a fast growing field. The engineers developing browser technologies, devices, web standards, and programming languages are a busy bunch. Every year there a lot of changes. These changes add up, so that mastering web development takes longer and longer. Meanwhile, websites depreciate.


Business owners are a productive bunch, with savvy skills-sets that include leadership and learning on the fly. While some owners are happy to leave the details to the developers, others want to know everything about their website. It helps to be in control when something breaks.


But there is just so much to learn about web development - and more and more every year - an owner can get distracted from their core business, and spend too much time learning web development.


But there is another way!


The mission of this newsletter is to make it easier for website owners to learn more about their websites, gradually.


We aim to keep you updated about technology and industry changes that may apply to you.


My own goal as a writer is to present relevant information in an easily understandable way, and to make reading this newsletter fun!


Sounds like a lot of work doesn't it! You may be wondering how I plan on getting paid!


Well, full disclosure. Currently Build Hello is just a name. We're not yet an agency.


I get paid when you decide to hire me to work or consult on your website.


So, while there's no obligation to pay to read the newsletter - it's just free information, sent to any e-mail you sign up with - there will be occasional reminders of technologies and services in the market place, and offers from us!


Thanks for reading! See you in your inbox!
