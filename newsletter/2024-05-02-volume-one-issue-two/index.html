Grants

If you're a canadian-based business, or your US-based business has a canadian subsidiary, you may qualify for the Canada Digital Adoption Program (CDAP).

Under CDAP, you can apply to 'get up to $2,400 to help you reach your customers where they are,' and/or up to $15,000 in advice and $100,000 in interest free loans.

That's fresh money you can use to:

 ‌
• Rebrand
• Launch a social media campaign
• Launch a SEO campaign
• Design and build templates
• Install a store
• Improve your analytics
• Develop a video strategy
 ‌

Contact us at www.buildhello.ca if you'd like some new ideas.
