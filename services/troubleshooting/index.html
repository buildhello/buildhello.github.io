Maintenance

Websites crash for all kinds of reasons:

  • Browsers and operating systems release new versions.

  • Frameworks release updates that conflict with packages and plugins.

  • Package authors move on and stop maintaining packages.

  • Languages themselves are constantly improving.

  • Any change to any input in your technology stack or code base can crash your site, or reveal flaws in your code.


These errors are often very visible. Even one tiny error can break your entire site.

But the worst errors are errors you can't see.

When users navigate your website using different technologies, they should experience what you planned for them to experience, not a wonky, broken, website.

Adding a monthly maintenance package from a local web development service provider is a cost effective way to stay on top of potential issues.

It's also intelligent to have your code base properly assessed. A decent report outlining your web site architecture and indicating areas where routine errors should be anticipated is the next best thing to having your developer include an easy to navigate professionally written users manual in your initial web site development.

Having said that, sometimes you just want to get up and running again as quickly as possible.

At Build Hello, we can help you with all of the above.

We'll let you know what prices to expect before any work is done, and tell what the minimum of what needs to be done to get back up and running as quickly as possiible.

We also give recommendations for changes that improve your website in several areas, including: search engine optimization, design, code base refactoring, improvements to plugin (package) selection, and more.

When your website isn't helping, give us a call. We'll get you looking your best again so you can get back being a rockstar.
