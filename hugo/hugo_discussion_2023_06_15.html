Build Hello Hugo Discussion - 2023-06-15

What is Hugo?

^

  • Command line program
  • Frontmatter CMS, and other CMS, so you can work with it with less complexity
  • Typically free hosting, for brochure and blog sites

What is a brochure site?

^

  • Brochure sites:
  • Don’t change often (updated once every few months?)
  • Little interactivity
  • 1 - 100 pages
  • Most businesses with location or community driven business have brochure sites
  • Example: law firm, hair salon, accountancy, grocery store

^

  • Brochure site prices:
  • Anywhere from $250 - $50,000

What are blog sites?

^

  • Any site that changes often
  • Typically as often as possible
  • SEO campaign (you want Google traffic)
  • Can be posted to Facebook or Linkedin

^

  • Blog site prices:
  • Anywhere from $350 - $50,000 +

What are membership sites?

^

  • Any site with greater complexity
  • Sign-in
  • Store
  • Downloads, etc

^

  • Membership site prices:
  • Anywhere from $5,000 - $1,000,000 +

Who uses Hugo?

  • Surprisingly wide variety of users
  • Academia
  • Sciences
  • Tech
  • Knowledge work, accounting firms, architects,
  • Artist, incidentally

Why is Hugo popular?

  • Modern brand
  • Produces lightweight free to host sites
  • Structure is (arguably) easier to comprehend

Hugo compared to Wordpress

^

  • Hosting Hugo usually free for blog and brochure
  • Hosting Wordpress usually costs for large brochure, and blog
  • Hosting Wordpress brochure can be free, if you’re careful

^

  • Hugo, offers a great developer experience, once you know what you’re doing, but not consistent
  • Wordpress offers a consistent developer experience
  • Under the hood, Hugo is often conceptually easier to grasp for many non-techies

^

  • Wordpress originated when it had fewer competitors
  • A lot of effort went into developing the UI and plugin and play functionality
  • In that sense, Hugo is still developing and behind Wordpress

^

  • For example, as a non-techie user, you can install/uninstall plugins in Wordpress with relative ease
  • The same functionality, is typically hard coded into a theme in Hugo
  • That’s something to be aware of. It means, hey, use this but if you want to make a gazillion changes, hire developer

^

  • In Wordpress, while some people enjoy learning it, and have the time, if you specialize elsewhere, you should also hire developer
